Frequently asked questions

If your question is not here, contact us and we will help.

How do I schedule an appointment with a therapist?
You can request an appointment by filling out the form below or calling our office. We follow up to learn what you are looking for and help match you with a therapist who fits your needs.
What should I expect in my first therapy session?
Your first session is a chance to get to know each other. You will talk about what brings you in, what you hope to change, and any questions you have.
Do you prescribe medication?
Our primary focus is psychotherapy. While we do not prescribe medication directly, we often collaborate with psychiatrists and primary care providers to ensure coordinated care.
How long are the sessions?
Standard individual therapy sessions are typically 50-55 minutes. Intake sessions or family sessions may sometimes be longer, depending on the clinician and your needs.
