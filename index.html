Frequently asked questions

If your question is not here, contact us and we will help.

How do I schedule an appointment with a therapist?
You can request an appointment by filling out the form below or calling our office. We follow up to learn what you are looking for and help match you with a therapist who fits your needs, preferences, and availability.
What should I expect in my first therapy session?
Your first session is a chance to get to know each other. You will talk about what brings you in, what you hope to change, and any questions you have. There is no pressure to share everything right away. We move at a pace that feels safe and doable.
How does using my insurance work?
If you want to use insurance, we gather your details and verify benefits so you know what to expect. If we are not in network with your plan, we can discuss out of network options and documentation.
How much does therapy cost if I am paying out of pocket?
Self pay fees are typically in the $100 to $175 range depending on the provider and service. We will be clear about cost before you commit, and talk through options if finances are a concern.
What is the cancellation or rescheduling policy?
We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations and rescheduling. Late cancellations may be charged a fee. We understand life happens and your therapist will do their best to work with you when something unexpected comes up.
